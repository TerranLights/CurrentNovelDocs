Four realms/civilizations, one per book:
- **King** — land-based civilization
- **Warrior** — a technological, mathematical version of ancient Sparta
- **Magician** — "basically in the wild"; a candidate for being the series' one ethereal/simulated realm (see Blocking Decisions)
- **Lover** — seafaring/maritime civilization, communicates entirely through symbol and song, no spoken language; the other candidate for being the ethereal/simulated realm

Exactly one of the four is intended to be metaphysical/ethereal (reinterpreted, since this is now a Tepenian-universe story, as some kind of simulation) — not yet decided which. See `structure/Dev-Road-Map/02-Blocking-Decisions.md`.

Placement within the Tepenian universe (Earth-side in a specific historical era, vs. off-world/space) is not yet decided. See `structure/Dev-Road-Map/00-Overview.md`.
