# Blocking Decisions (Series Level)

Decisions that must be resolved before substantive work can proceed in the areas they affect.

## Decision 1: Lover vs. Magician as the Ethereal/Simulated Realm

**Status: OPEN — highest priority in this series**

Confirmed (2026-07-08, via research) that the King/Warrior/Magician/Lover archetype names come from Robert Moore & Douglas Gillette's real book, but the Father/Mother, physical/metaphysical split imposed on top of them does not exist in that book — it's the author's own invention, free to redesign. Current direction: exactly one realm is ethereal/simulated, the other three physical. Full arguments for both Lover and Magician are written up in `four-realm musician - architecture options (Lover vs Magician).md` at the `CurrentNovelDocs` repo root (outside this project folder, since it predates this folder's creation).

**Downstream effects:**
- Blocks full development of whichever book turns out to be the ethereal one — its Dev-Road-Map can't be filled in with confidence until this is settled
- Blocks Phase 1 (Foundations) for the series as a whole
