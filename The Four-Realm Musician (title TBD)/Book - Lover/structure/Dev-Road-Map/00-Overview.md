# The Four-Realm Musician — Book: Lover — Development Roadmap

## What This Is

Book-level roadmap for the Lover volume. See the series-level roadmap at `../../structure/Dev-Road-Map/` for series-wide status and shared context.

---

## Current State

*As of 2026-07-08 — the richest-developed of the four realms so far, thanks to strong seed material, though still very early.*

**Overall completion: ~8%**

What is solid:
- Seafaring/maritime civilization; communicates entirely through symbol and song, no spoken language
- Lover is one of the two candidates for the series' one ethereal/simulated realm (see series Blocking Decision 1) — the other candidate, on the strength of directly-connected seed material: the 2019 CD-R dream (wordless music, can't carry it home) and the poem "Seaside Chorus" (`../../reference/reference articles/Seaside Chorus (poem).md`) — a solitary guitarist, wordless, on a moonlit beach, joined by a mysterious "ghostly note"
- Counter-argument against Lover being the ethereal one: Moore & Gillette define the Lover archetype as "the energy that connects one to others and the world," arguably the most grounded/relational of the four — see the full writeup for both sides

What is incomplete:
1. Whether this realm is physical or simulated — the single highest-priority open item for this specific book
2. This book's own artifact and what retrieving it involves
3. Any named characters within this civilization
4. Full beat-by-beat plot structure
