Atop the operation chair in Ric's
Garage sat Valerie, her robot arm
Exposed. Ricardo sorted out the nicks
And tears as Craig and Martin sat. A swarm
Of vapor puffed from Valerie as Craig,
Beside her, sat and held her other hand.
His older brother Martin spoke: "So, vague
Idea, how soon can she make her grand
Return?" he asked. Ricardo, head a-tilt,
Responded, "Hard to say. The damage went
In further than I'd figured. She was built
Real tough, though. Damage isn't permanent."
With hands still squeezing, robot and evolved,
Another day, another problem solved.
