The sun was sliding down the western sky as Craig
and Martin readied for the trek to Monterey:
A tent and sleeping bags for somewhere near the bay
tomorrow, an extension cable with a vague
idea where to plug it in for Valerie,
whatever canned food Craig and Martin could agree
on, with a stash of siligel and water prepped,
the brothers went and rested on the patio.
Inside the bedroom, 'neath the iridescent glow
of lava lamps and sunshine, Valerie's adept
robotic fingers bent and twisted metal bits
of steel and copper inasmuch as it permits.
Her girlish little hands, though metal-fortified
themselves,
								slide
each bit and wire into place to form a shape.
