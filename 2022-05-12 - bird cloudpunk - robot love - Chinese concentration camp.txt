At first, I was in a cloudpunk / solarpunk setting. We travelled from place to place inside of birds. They were large, blue and/or grey birds. There were cities both on the ground and floating cities in the air. The birds were probably made of ultra-light metal or graphene or something. There were huMans and gynoids.

Next thing I know, I've landed at one city and got assigned to a sidequest Fallout-style for a local faction. The local robots need me to find something for them. I meet one particular gynoid who's clearly mine. I'd never met her before this moment, but I already "know" her in a way I'm unable to explain. She's not a living robotic representation of any doll I already have on my real-world wishlist. Rather, she's a completely new gynoid. She's an original. She's thicc and stacked, has ersatz blue-green and purple-ish hair, and has shimmering, glowing yellow eyes. I promise her I'll find what the local robot faction needs. She snuggles into me and says she appreciates me. I hug her adoringly, kiss her, and tell her I love her. I then kiss her again and depart to go on the faction quest for this city of robots.

Next thing I know, I've been tracked down by the Chinese and put into a concentration camp like the ones in Anchorage in the Fallout universe. They sit me on a chair, tie my hands behind me with my arms extended sideways, both to my left, tied to a wooden frame. They then waterboard me to try to make me talk. I don't. I'd rather die than sell out the robots. I'm then stung in the back of the neck by a stingwing, and I wake up.
