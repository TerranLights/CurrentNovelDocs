Setting shifts across the series:
1. Initially: Midwestland
2. Later: Greater New North America
3. Finally: South America, then the Falkland Islands

Ends at the historical hinge point of the shared universe: the War of Upper Earth and the signing of the Falkland Treaty (June 21, 2564), which establishes the Federation of Tepenia and exiles robots to Antarctica. See `reference/story-structure-definitions.md` and the shared universe-timeline reference for the full historical context this series leads directly into.
