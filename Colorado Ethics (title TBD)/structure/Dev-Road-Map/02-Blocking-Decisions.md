# Blocking Decisions

Decisions that must be resolved before substantive work can proceed in the areas they affect. None of these are questions with an obvious right answer — they are choices that have been left open, and leaving them open would mean building downstream work on undefined ground.

**What qualifies as a blocking decision (not just an open question):** something a later phase genuinely cannot proceed past without an answer.

**What does NOT qualify as a blocking decision:**
- **A character not yet having a proper name.** Logged below anyway, alongside the fuller character-design gap — the blocker is the missing design, not the missing name.
- **Theme not yet being clear.** Not logged here — see `07-Phase-5-Theme.md`.
