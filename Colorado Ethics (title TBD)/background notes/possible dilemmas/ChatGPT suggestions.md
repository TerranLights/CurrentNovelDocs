Question:

what are some possible dilemmas that humans would be perfectly fine with but robots would view as unethical and immoral?



Answer:

Surveillance and Privacy Invasion
Human Perspective: Many humans accept widespread surveillance in the name of national security or personal convenience, such as surveillance cameras in public spaces, data mining by corporations, or even government monitoring.
Robot Perspective: A robot with a strong sense of autonomy, privacy, and individual rights might see these actions as deeply unethical. They might view the infringement on privacy and individual freedoms as a violation of personal dignity and rights, believing that individuals should have the autonomy to control their personal information and space without undue intrusion.
